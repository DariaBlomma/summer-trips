New words

The continuation of the story

First, read and translate the questions. Write down the new words not to forget them.

Then, look through the text and find the places where the answers are. Mark these places - you will need to understand them very well.

After that, start translating. Pay special attention to the marked places. Maybe, you will have to translate more words there.

Do not translate every unknown word. If you can understand, even approximately, the meaning of a paragraph, go further.

If you feel, that some details are important, but you don't understand them, translate some words.

The less you translate - the better. You won't enjoy reading if you translate every single word
