When my mother came home, we (to have) dinner

Variant 1. Past Simple

The actions happened one after the other

1. My mother came 2. We (with my mum) had dinner

Variant 2. Past Continuous

The 2nd action was happening during the 1st action

We started eating, then the mother came home and saw us eating (without her). She got sad and had to eat without us

Variant 3. Past Perfect

The 2nd action happened before the 1st action

We ate dinner (without my mum), then the mother came and saw empty, dirty dishes and no food

Variant 4. Past Perfect Continous

The 2nd action has been happening for some time before the 1st action

We had been eating dinner for 2 hours (it was my birthday and we had guests), then my mother came and saw half-empty dishes and a loud crowd of guests
